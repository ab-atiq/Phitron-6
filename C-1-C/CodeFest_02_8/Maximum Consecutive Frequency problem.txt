ЁЯУЭ Problem Statement:

A consecutive frequency means the number of times a value repeats continuously without interruption.

Your job is to:
1. Find the number with the highest consecutive frequency.
2. Print that number and its maximum frequency.

If two or more numbers have the same consecutive frequency, choose the one that appears first in the array.
